    <summary type="html">&lt;!-- [DocumentBodyStart:2d991518-099d-44cc-895d-e6929a0bf12d] --&gt;&lt;div class="jive-rendered-content"&gt;&lt;p style="margin-bottom: 1em;"&gt;It been quiet this week, because many people did attend&lt;a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://www.redhat.com/summit/"&gt; JBoss World&lt;/a&gt; and &lt;a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://www.jboss.org/events/JUDCon/2012/boston"&gt;JUDCon&lt;/a&gt; in Boston. &lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;Red Hat has done a number of acquisitions over the years, including JBoss and Gluster. Well it's with great pleasure that for us to&amp;#160; announce another: &lt;a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://fusesource.com/"&gt;FuseSource&lt;/a&gt;. You can find the official press release &lt;a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://www.redhat.com/about/news/press-archive/2012/6/red-hat-to-acquire-fusesource"&gt;here&lt;/a&gt;. &lt;/p&gt;&lt;p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;"&gt;&amp;#160;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;h3&gt;New Releases&lt;/h3&gt;&lt;p style="min-height: 8pt; height: 8pt; padding: 0px;"&gt;&amp;#160;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p style="margin-bottom: 1em;"&gt;&lt;span style="font-style: inherit;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;&lt;a class="jive-link-external-small" href="http://ceylon-lang.org/blog/2012/06/25/ceylon-m3-v2000/" target="_blank"&gt;Ceylon M3 and Ceylon IDE M3 released!&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;br/&gt;Ceylon M3 "V2000" is now available for download, along with a simultaneous compatible release of Ceylon IDE. The first three Ceylon platform modules are available in Ceylon Herd, the community module repository.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p style="margin-bottom: 1em;"&gt;&lt;span style="font-style: inherit;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;&lt;a class="jive-link-blog-small" href="https://community.jboss.org/community/portletbridge/blog/2012/06/22/jboss-portlet-bridge-231final-released" target="_blank"&gt;JBoss Portlet Bridge 2.3.1.Final Released&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;br/&gt;JBoss Portlet Bridge 2.3.1.Final is now available.
